Dream Interpretations  Dreams about: sex  

Sex

Interpretation: Sex is the most powerful instinct in the human psyche. In dreams, it tends to satisfy a natural desire when sex is absent in waking life. With someone you like platonically: It is an affirmation of a close relationship. With someone you do not like: It is time to moderate those negative feelings about them. With the same sex: It may be yourself trying to deal with your male/ female counterpart or the part of you that you do not like.

Sex (intercourse)

Interpretation: It is very difficult to name just a few possible interpretations for this dream. It is so complex that interpretations vary with each dreamer and situation in the dream. A sexual dream may be about physical pleasure, but it may also be about power, control, manipulation, virility, and effectiveness. It may be a form of wish fulfillment or a memory, or compensation for a lack in daily life. In most cases, it is not a prediction of things to come in the near future.

Sextuplets

Interpretation: A dream about sextuplets suggests that your mental, emotional and spiritual states are coming into harmony. You may also be experiencing positive changes that are making your home life more happy.
